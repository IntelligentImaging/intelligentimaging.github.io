<div class="prose field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field--mode-full field--item"><p>Maria Camila Cortes-Albornoz is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Massachusetts General Hospital. She was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Computational Radiology Laboratory at Boston Children's Hospital from April 2022 to April 2023. She received her MD from Universidad del Rosario, Colombia, where she also worked in neuroscience research at NeuroVitae Center. Camila is interested in applying novel MRI techniques to fetal and neonatal imaging to advance in brain development understanding.</p>
